Bio

Iason Svoronos - Kanavas is a Master of Science by Research student in Computer Science and Informatics at the Applied Psychoacoustics Lab (APL), School of Computing & Engineering, University of Huddersfield, UK. He has a background in audio engineering (BSc, Ionian University, GR) and sonification, the process of mapping data onto non-speech sound for the purpose of enhancing perception and understanding.

Under the supervision of Prof. Hyunkook Lee, the research project Soni-Cross, focuses on the design, development, and evaluation of a proposed Acoustic Vehicle Alerting System (AVAS) model. The evaluation specifically focuses on the impact of applying different frequency band modes (UN R138) to the model on perceived annoyance. Additionally, the project investigates the effects of audiovisual simulations in the evaluation of AVAS, comparing these with audio-only testing to assess differences in annoyance perception.
