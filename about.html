Professional

Software Engineer at JOYNEXT with focus on Embedded Systems in automotive infotainment.

I have been awarded with a Master of Science by Research in Computer Science and Informatics from the University of Huddersfield, UK. My study, supervised by Prof. Hyunkook Lee at the Applied Psychoacoustics Lab and funded by SYN-ENOSIS and University of Huddersfield, explored the impact of different frequency band modes (UN-R138) in AVAS for EVs on annoyance perception using a novel VR evaluation framework. The thesis will be published under the title: Exploring the Influence of Frequency Components in Acoustic Vehicle Alerting Systems for Electric Vehicles on Annoyance Perception. Follow the Link for more information.

I hold a BSc Degree in Sound Technology from the Ionian University in Greece with a specialisation in sonification, the process of mapping data onto non-speech sound for the purpose of enhancing perception and understanding.
