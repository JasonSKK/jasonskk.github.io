Cinematic Transition 210402


This cinematic transition is a intuitive approach to the common elements of the physical word (nodes from the tree branches) with the neurons of the human brain.
The general idea is focused on the similarity of the forms (ie in a schematic way) and additionally in the common way of operation between the physical object (ie the tree) and the human brain. More specifically, the system of transporting nutrients from the soil to the tree through a "network". In some sense, the human brain works in a similar way regarding the neuron system in which signals are triggered by corresponding stimuli.

Sound synthesis was implemented in the framework of SuperCollider.
