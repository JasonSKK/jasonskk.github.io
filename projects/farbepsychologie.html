~ Farbe Psychologie ~




A Few Thoughts ...

Collage Art - Multimedia presentation 210307.


This collage piece is entitled "Farbe Psychologie".

Inspiration was derived from by a research paper that studies the perception of colours and emotions they cause us. This paper is entitled "Colours and its significance".

Tavaragi, Dr & C, Mrs. (2016). Colors and Its Significance. International Journal of Indian Psychology. 3. https://doi.org/10.25215/0302.126.



It was decided to present the collage in class with a video.

The collage is dominated by 3 colours, red, yellow and grey. Emphasis is also placed on a personal view, ie how the creator perceives the specific colours emotionally.
More specifically:

Red: Is related to the energy, strength but also aggression.

Yellow: Joy and optimism.

Grey: Vanity, sadness, lack of confidence and depression.


Each emotion is described through a selected written text.
1. Erik Satie, Εκτός ήχου (greek translation).
2. Lord George Gordon Byron, "Σήμερα έκλειστα τα 36 μου χρόνια.
3. William Butler Yeats, "The wheel".

The audio was created in the SuperCollider framework and it was based mostly on additive synthesis techniques.
